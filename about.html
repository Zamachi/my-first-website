About us

"This website was made as a presentation for a web design class, therefore this website isn't monetised by any means. The idea behind it is to present the author's knowledge base of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery. Website's theme is concentrated around computer hardware. The computer hardware that is presented includes: monitors, fans, PC cases, mice and keyboards. Features of the website are many: sliders, dropdown lists, parallax background, forms, a FAQ list, Google maps, "night-mode" switch etc. "

-Author
